Yogurt and Honey Fruit Cups

requirements- you need 4-1/2 cups cut-up fresh fruit (pears, apples, bananas, grapes, etc.), 3/4 cup mandarin orange, vanilla or lemon yogurt, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/2 teaspoon grated orange zest, 1/4 teaspoon almond extract

Recipe

Divide fruit among 6 individual serving bowls. Combine the yogurt, honey, orange zest and extract; spoon over the fruit. 97 calories, 0 fat, 23g carbohydrate, 2g protein.
